🌟 About

Anastasia Salter is Professor of English and Director of Graduate Programs in the College of Arts and Humanities at the University of Central Florida, and President of the Electronic Literature Organization. Her work spans game studies, electronic literature, digital humanities, feminist media studies, critical making, and the cultural stakes of generative AI. She is the author or co-author of ten books and the creator of award-nominated works of electronic literature.
